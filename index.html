Key brand rules

Three brand colors
Main Blue #1E67B2, Main Teal #10BAB3, Main Green #81BF41 — CTAs use primary blue.
8px rounded buttons
All buttons use border-radius: 8px for consistent rounded corners.
Montserrat + Noto Sans
Headings always use Montserrat. Body text always uses Noto Sans. Never swap these.

Brand voice

Professional yet warm — trustworthy, accessible, community-rooted, and empowering.

Access
Making clinical research accessible to everyone, everywhere.
Trust
Safety-first protocols and transparent communication.
Community
A network of sites, physicians, and participants connected by purpose.
Impact
Advancing medicine through rigorous, patient-centered research.
